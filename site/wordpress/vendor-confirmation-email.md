# Vendor confirmation email — flat $300 setup

Paste this into your WordPress form plugin's confirmation/notification
template (WPForms, Elementor Forms, Gravity Forms, etc.). Replace the
`{...}` merge tags with your plugin's equivalents and fill the two
`[BRACKETED]` items. There are no per-day price tiers anymore — do not
re-add any per-day pricing language.
