Meet our ambassador

Tricia Downing

Wheelchair Division Athlete Ambassador

Last year Tricia made history as the first wheelchair athlete to compete in the Santa Fe International Half Marathon — and made sure this race belongs to athletes of every ability. We couldn't be happier to have her back.

After a cycling accident left her paralyzed from the chest down, Tricia found her way back through adaptive sports — and then kept going. She was the first female wheelchair athlete to complete an Iron-distance triathlon. These days she's a Paralympic shooter, training with her sights set on representing the United States at the 2028 Los Angeles Paralympic Games.

She's also an author, keynote speaker, disability advocate, and bicultural adoptee. Her book, Cycle of Hope: A Journey from Paralysis to Possibility, tells the whole story better than we can here.

This year, catch Tricia speaking at the Health & Wellness Expo. Come hear her story — it's the best warm-up you'll get all weekend.
