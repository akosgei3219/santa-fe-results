What's New This Year

A few things we're especially excited about for this edition.

Finish-line food by Santa Fe YouthWorks

Your post-race meal at Railyard Park is prepared by the crew at Santa Fe YouthWorks — free for runners. Place your order ahead of time and it's ready when you cross the line.

Tricia Downing returns

Our Wheelchair Division Athlete Ambassador is back — and she's speaking at the Health & Wellness Expo. Come hear her story.

Live results, the moment you finish

Chip times post to the live leaderboard every 30 seconds on race day. Type your bib number and find your time before you've caught your breath.

Past Champions wall

The fastest man and woman from every edition, pulled straight from the official results — and this year's winners join them the day they cross the line.

Post-race celebrations at Nuckolls Brewing

The runner happy-hour pint is back — and so are the $5 pretzels. Cross the line, grab your medal, and come celebrate with us.
